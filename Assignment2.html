Australian Climate and the 2019-20 Bushfire Season

Rain and Aridity

Fire spread is based heavily on the availability of dry fuel - areas with heavy rainfall are less likely to be affected by fire spread, and areas with low maximum temperatures are likely to be dry less often

Examine rainfall and temperature as recorded at many meteorology stations and compare it to the area in which fire was detected in the 2019-20 bushfire season

Temperatures

Australian "Bushfire Season" occurs as temperatures and wind speeds climb into the summer. In 2019, fire wardens warned that bushfire season may start as early as July.

Explore bushfire seasons year on year by setting a cut-off for maximum temperatures

Fire Spread

The 2019-20 bushfire season, colloquially referred to as "black summer" or the "february fires", was one of the worst on record and had widespread news coverage.
